Tracy's Favorite Trip

My Honeymoon in Costa Rica!

Here are a few highlights of my favorite trip!

Arenal

My husband and I spent five days in Costa Rica, staying at two different resorts in the area of the Arenal Volcano. Arenal is a long inactive volcano, but a breathtaking sight to see!

The Peace Lodge

The Peace Lodge was the first resort where we stayed. This resort is attached to La Paz Waterfall gardens. You can talk a walk on trails cutting through the rainforest and look at the amazing waterfalls. The resort also had a small zoo area. I enjoyed seeing all the animals. While we were staying here, we got to do a few fun activities:

  • Visited Poas Volcano (another great volcano that is actually mostly underground)
  • Visited a coffee plantation
  • Took a nightime tour of an animal preserve

The Lost Iguana Resort

Our second resort was The Lost Iguana. With a view of the Arenal Volcano from our patio, this was certainly another memorable stay. The resort was fantastic and we were able to do some more exciting things like:

  • Zip-lining through the canopy
  • Conopy hiking tour
  • Touring the Arenal Volcano
  • Swimming in natural hot springs

Overall Experience

Costa Rica was my favorite trip for a few reasons. One is the spectacular views. Being in rain forest, you are surrounded by nature. Also, being an animal person, I was ecstatic about getting to see and touch may different animals I wouldn't have been able to here. The food was amazing. We definitely did not go hungry. Lastly, the people are so warm and inviting. I can't wait until I can go again!
